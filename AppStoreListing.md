## Description

Trakt is a training and nutrition tracker built around one idea: your calorie target shouldn't be
a fixed number that ignores whether you just crushed a leg day or took a rest day.

**Train**
- Curated programs (Full-Body, Push/Pull/Legs, 5/3/1, Upper/Lower) or build your own — custom days, exercises, sets, reps, and weight, with optional scheduled deloads.
- Log sets against a 873-exercise library, with weight/rep suggestions based on your last session's performance.
- A rest timer that survives backgrounding, RPE tracking, and mid-session editing if the gym doesn't have what your program calls for.

**Eat**
- Search a combined USDA, Open Food Facts, and FatSecret database, or log manually.
- Editable portions before anything gets logged — no guessing serving sizes.
- Macros that scale with your actual training load that day, not a flat number.

**Progress**
- Bodyweight trend, weekly workout adherence, real nutrition insight (not just a hit/miss streak).
- Lift-by-lift progression charts.

**Why it's different**
Your daily calorie target moves with your training — a heavy day earns a higher target, a rest day
doesn't. It's built on Mifflin-St Jeor TDEE math with a safety floor, not an arbitrary guess, and
every adjustment is explained, not just applied silently.

Free. No ads, no paywall, no subscription.
